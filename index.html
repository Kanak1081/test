India Bans Chinese Apps

Government of India has banned 59 apps originating from China,including very popular ones like the short video platform TikTok, UC Browser, file sharing app ShareIt and CamScanner,etc. This action could be a warning to bigger Chinese businesses in India and to China itself.

Statistics Day

Statistics Day of India is being celebrated on June 29,2020 on the birth anniversary of Prof. P C Mahalanobis. He was an Indian scientist and applied statistician. He established the Indian Statistical Institute (ISI) in 1993 in Kolkata.

Covaxin

Covaxin, India's first vaccine candidate against novel coronavirus, developed by vaccine maker Bharat Biotech gets the approval of Drug Controller General of India(DCGI) for Phase I and Phase II clinical trials. It is an inactivated vaccine,created from a strain of infectious SARS-CoV-2 virus,that has shown promise in preclinical studies, demonstrating extensive safety and effective immune responses.

International Day of the Tropics

The International Day of the Tropics 2020 was celebrated on June 29. The Tropics are the region of the Earth,roughly defined as the area between the Tropic of Cancer and Tropic of Capricorn. The day aims to raise awareness to the specific challenges faced by tropical areas.
